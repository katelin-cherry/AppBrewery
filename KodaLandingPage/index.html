For Patients

At any age, a medical crisis could leave you too ill to make your own healthcare decisions. Although it's difficult and often overwhelming to talk, families who have these discussions with their physicians can confidently communicate their loved one's wishes with medical personnel during an emergency.

You're entitled to protect the healthcare decisions that matter most to your and your family. Koda creates Care Plans to ensure that you get the healthcare you want.
